Last updated: August 21, 2025

Auto-Promoter ("we," "our," or "us") is committed to protecting your privacy. This Privacy Policy explains how we collect, use, and safeguard your information when you use our social media automation platform.

1. Information We Collect

1.1 Personal Information

  • Business Information: Company name, website URL, business description, target audience, and keywords
  • Contact Information: Email address and business contact details
  • Social Media Accounts: URLs and handles for your social media profiles

1.2 API Configuration Data

  • Social Media API Keys: Access tokens, app IDs, and page IDs for Facebook, Instagram, YouTube, LinkedIn, and TikTok

1.3 Usage Data

  • Content Generation: AI-generated posts and marketing content
  • Analytics: Post performance metrics and engagement data

2. How We Use Your Information

2.1 Core Services

  • Content Generation: Create AI-powered marketing content based on your business information
  • Social Media Automation: Schedule and post content to your connected social media platforms

2.2 Platform Integration

  • API Management: Securely store and manage your social media API credentials
  • Cross-Platform Posting: Enable posting to multiple social media platforms simultaneously

3. Data Storage and Security

3.1 Storage

  • Local Storage: API configurations and business data are stored locally in your browser

  • No Permanent Storage: We do not permanently store your API keys or business data on our servers

3.2 Security Measures

  • Encryption: All sensitive data is encrypted in transit
  • Access Control: Limited access to your data by authorized personnel only
  • Regular Audits: Security practices are regularly reviewed and updated

4. Data Sharing and Disclosure

We do not sell, trade, or otherwise transfer your personal information to third parties, except:

5.1 Access and Control

5.2 Opt-Out Options

6. Data Retention

We retain your information only as long as necessary to provide our services:
