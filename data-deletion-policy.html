Data Deletion Policy

Last updated: August 21, 2025

Auto-Promoter is committed to protecting your privacy and ensuring you have full control over your data. This Data Deletion Policy explains how you can request the deletion of your personal information and what happens during the deletion process.

1. Your Right to Data Deletion

Under various privacy laws and regulations, you have the right to request the deletion of your personal information. Auto-Promoter respects this right and provides multiple ways for you to exercise it.

2. What Data We Store

2.1 Business Information

  • Company name and description
  • Website URL and business details
  • Target audience and keywords
  • Contact information

2.2 Social Media Configuration

  • Social media account URLs and handles
  • API keys and access tokens
  • Platform-specific settings
  • Posting preferences and schedules

2.3 Generated Content

  • AI-generated marketing posts
  • Content templates and variations
  • Posting history and analytics
  • Performance metrics and insights

3. How to Request Data Deletion

3.1 Email Request (Recommended)

1 Send Email: Email us at raahuketu8@gmail.com
2 Subject Line: "Delete My Data - Auto-Promoter"
3 Include: Your business name and email address used for registration
4 Optional: Specify which data you want deleted (all data or specific categories)

3.2 Platform-Based Deletion

  • Clear Local Storage: Clear your browser's local storage to remove locally stored data
  • Remove API Keys: Delete your API configurations from the platform
  • Reset Business Info: Clear your business information fields

4. Data Deletion Process

4.1 Immediate Actions

  • Account Deactivation: Your account is immediately deactivated
  • API Access Removal: All API connections are immediately disabled
  • Content Generation Stop: AI content generation is immediately halted

4.2 Data Removal Timeline

  • Within 24 hours: All active sessions and API connections are terminated
  • Within 7 days: All stored business information and configurations are removed
  • Within 30 days: All generated content and analytics data are permanently deleted
  • Within 60 days: All backup data and logs are permanently removed

5. What Happens After Deletion

5.1 Immediate Effects

  • You can no longer access your account
  • All automated posting stops immediately
  • Generated content is no longer accessible
  • API connections are permanently disabled

5.2 Permanent Removal

  • All business information is permanently deleted
  • Social media configurations are completely removed
  • Generated content and templates are permanently erased
  • Analytics and performance data are completely wiped

6. Data Retention Exceptions

In certain circumstances, we may be required to retain some data:

  • Legal Requirements: Data required for legal compliance or regulatory purposes
  • Security Investigations: Data related to security incidents or fraud prevention
  • Service Recovery: Limited data for service restoration if requested

7. Verification Process

7.1 Identity Verification

To protect your privacy and prevent unauthorized deletion requests, we may need to verify your identity:

  • Email address verification
  • Business information confirmation
  • Additional security questions if necessary

7.2 Confirmation

Once your identity is verified and deletion is processed, you will receive:

  • Confirmation email with deletion details
  • Timeline for complete data removal
  • Contact information for any questions

8. Reversing Data Deletion

Important: Data deletion is permanent and cannot be reversed. Once your data is deleted, it cannot be recovered. Please ensure you have backed up any important information before requesting deletion.

9. Alternative Options

9.1 Data Export

Before deleting your data, you may want to export it:

  • Download your business information
  • Export generated content and templates
  • Save your social media configurations
  • Backup your analytics and performance data

9.2 Account Deactivation

Instead of complete deletion, you can:

  • Deactivate your account temporarily
  • Remove specific data categories
  • Disable automated posting features
  • Keep your account for future use
